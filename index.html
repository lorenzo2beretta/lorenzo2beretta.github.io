Lorenzo Beretta


Hi! I am Lorenzo and I am currently a postdoc at University of California, Santa Cruz hosted by Vaggos Chatziafratis. In September, I am going to join IBM as a Goldstine fellow. I recently graduated from BARC, University of Copenhagen, where I was fortunate to be advised by Mikkel Thorup and Mikkel Abrahamsen.

I am a theoretical computer scientist and I study algorithms for massive datasets. My work focuses on sublinear algorithms, property testing, and high-dimensional computational geometry.
